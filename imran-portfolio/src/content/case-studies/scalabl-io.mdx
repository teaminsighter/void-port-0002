# Scalabl.io

A deep dive into how I designed and built Scalabl.io — a GoHighLevel-style all-in-one business platform featuring CRM, funnels, email marketing, automation, and AI-powered workflows.

## The Problem

Businesses juggle 10+ different tools to manage their operations. This fragmentation leads to data silos, inefficiency, and wasted budget.

## The Solution

A unified platform that brings everything under one roof, powered by AI workflows that automate repetitive tasks.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, PostgreSQL
- **Payments**: Stripe
- **AI**: Custom AI workflow engine
